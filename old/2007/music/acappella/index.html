A Cappella music

This page contains music for Chorda Tympani (or any other a cappella group, for that matter). The idea is to sing along with the audio files while using sheet music. For learning, just download the appropriate part in MP3 format. They have beginning and ending rests cut out, and you'll only hear your part. Practice! The whole song is also available, once you're ready.

The NoteWorthy files are really just for editing the sheet music, though you can use them with the free NoteWorthy Composer Viewer if you'd like to watch the music play along or print it. They're not the prettiest, but they work. I haven't tried the viewer personally, so let me know if there are any specific problems with these songs.

If you have any concerns, comments, questions, or complaints, please feel free to contact me.
