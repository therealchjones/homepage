Components of ID
  • Name
  • Age
  • Sex
  • Marital status
  • Occupation
  • Ethnicity
  • Language (if not English)
  • Reliability
  • Informant (patient, chart, spouse, family member, “impression”)

Components of PMH
  • Lifestyle, exercise, sleep habits
  • Childhood & adult illnesses requiring medical treatment: Date, Diagnosis (Dx), treatment (Tx)
  • Significant injuries: Date, Dx, Tx
  • Surgical procedures: Date, Dx, Tx
  • Hospitalizations: Date, Dx, Tx
  • Immunizations, drug sensitivities, allergies
  • Med compliance

Components of Family Hx
  • Age & state of health of parents, siblings, spouse, and children
  • Hx of tuberculosis, DM, htn, heart attack, stroke, arthritis, cancer, alcohol, drug use, emotional problems
  • Hereditary disease, unusual illnesses, early deaths
  • Effects of patient health on family and vice-versa; "taboo" diseases like HIV
