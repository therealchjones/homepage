Still going…

12/04/2005 03:40:00 PM

Yeah, I'm still here. I got the PowerBook I wrote about, and it's fantastic. Sure enough, I've been working on turning it into my primary computer, but I don't really have enough time at the moment to even have a primary computer.

Who knew pediatrics would be time consuming? I really expected it to be similar to internal medicine, but that schedule was sweet. Here at CHLA, I'm working quite a bit, learning a ton, but it's taking a ton of time, too. I tend to get home and just crash instead of studying or doing extra work—I'm just drained when I get out of here. The teaching is wonderful, but time consuming—sometimes it would just be nice to get the work done and go home.

And speaking of going home, prolly isn't gonna happen anytime soon. I'm on call today, have finished all my work, and am updating my blog instead of studying while I wait for patients to show. My current supervisor has said I can go at 6 if we haven1t gotten any admissions… and look at that, as I was writing the last clause we got a transfer who's an “excellent learning opportunity” for me. See you later.
