I'm Getting a PowerBook!

11/14/2005 09:21:00 PM

That's right! After some negotiating with Lindsey (okay, there wasn't much negotiating—I believe the words “carte blanche” were the ones I used), I'm going to get my most expensive toy ever, a PowerBook—a new one even, with all the fixin's. It set me back a bit (okay, a lot), but it's got a 17-inch wide screen, five-and-a-half-hour battery life, 2 gigabytes of RAM, and a 100-gig 7200 RPM hard drive. Oh, not to mention the dual-layer 8x DVD burner, or all the other things you'd expect (or wouldn't). Okay, really, I'll try to stop bragging, but I'm freakin' thrilled. Don't expect more spending for a long time….
