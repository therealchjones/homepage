Back on nights…

10/12/2005 11:16:00 PM

For someone on night shifts, I'm getting a remarkable amount of sleep (which, I suppose, is why I'm remarking on it). Worked Monday 8 PM to 8 AM, had stuff to do during the day on Tuesday, and I'm back for 8 PM Wednesday to 8 AM Thursday—and I've gotten 16 hours of sleep in the last 24, 22 in the last 48. Not too much to complain about, really, and night shift certainly seems to pass more quickly.

Turns out Apple didn't announce new Power Macs and Powerbooks today, after all. I suppose I'm probably the only one bummed that they instead announced a video iPod. It's not that I wouldn't like one, but I'd really like a Powerbook, and feel I should wait until new configurations are announced. (Actually, I'd love a video iPod; I'd just like it to play MPEG-2 video and I'd like the iTunes videos to be at least television-resolution, preferably DVD.)

Okay, back to “work”.
