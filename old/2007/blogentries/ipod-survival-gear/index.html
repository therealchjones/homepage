iPod Survival Gear

5/03/2006 07:44:00 PM

Wow. I'm a technophile, but if I were asked what few things I'd require to survive for days trapped in a collapsed mine, I'm guessing my iPod still wouldn't be at the top of the list. Apparently not the case, however, for two trapped Tasmanian miners—they've been given iPods to get them through the crisis while rescue workers finish their “final push” to pull the miners out. No, it's not at all ridiculous.
