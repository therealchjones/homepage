24 hours, going strong

10/15/2005 06:58:00 AM

Yay! Not only because it's my last Obstetrics shift and it went well and I got great experience, but also because I've now been awake 25 hours and am becoming hypomanic. One more hour, then home and sweet, sweet sleep. Oh, and then I get to wake up and have barbecue and beer with my friend Pete. (No, not he of the Wicked Ale, but my other friend Pete.)

So good stuff last night, and it kept me awake well. Not only did I get my first real delivery, but I also got to scrub in on a C-section for a 24-week-old infant. Good stuff, and it made the time pass fairly quickly. I was even in a good enough mood to slack a little less this morning.

Ah, but one more hour…; and then I get a three (well, two and a half) day weekend and nothing but slack!
