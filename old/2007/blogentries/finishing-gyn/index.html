Finishing Gyn

10/31/2005 08:43:00 AM

Just about finished with the Ob/Gyn clerkship—in surgery today, call tomorrow, and then two days of studying before the big exam. The first surprise was this morning, though, when our pseudo-senior resident told me and my two colleagues that we'll likely receive Honors in the rotation if we do well enough on the written exam. Because of that last caveat, I'm not getting my hopes up too much, but it's nice (okay, amazing) to hear that I've done fairly well.

The big surprise, though? It's that I haven't hated it. I honestly expected Ob/Gyn to be the bane of my existence for six weeks, a circle of hell from which it was uncertain whether I'd emerge. It hasn't been fun, it's not something I'll do as a career, and I've been waking far too early, but I haven't hated it, and that's pretty amazing.

So, it turns out after my “time off” that I'm facing third year surprisingly well. I'll write more when I have time (man, I should really trademark that statement), but for now, it's back to the hoo-hahs.
