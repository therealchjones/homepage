Dell Inspiron 1100 For Sale

6/18/2006 08:48:00 PM

In an ongoing effort to simplify my life, I'm starting to get rid of some of my machines of questionable necessity. The first is my Dell Inspiron 1100 (service tag 39RW231). It's a nice little blue and gray notebook that has served me well, but is now mostly sitting around. Great shape, some minor scratches here and there on the outside edges, and a single cracked piece of plastic on the spare battery—this is all aesthetic damage only.

The hard specs:

  • Intel Celeron 2.0 GHz processor
  • 384 MB RAM (upgradeable to 1 GB)
  • 20 GB hard drive
  • 14.1 inch 1024x768 pixel display (no dead pixels)
  • DVD-ROM drive
  • Spare battery
  • Dell TrueMobile 1150 PC Card 802.11b wireless adapter

No software or operating system is included!

I don't really know what to charge; Internet used/refurbished laptop prices seem to be all over the place. Fry's tends to have bargain laptops (new) on sale for $450 to $500 usually, and they're certainly better than this one. Call it $300 or best offer. And by “best offer”, I actually mean it—serious offers of any amount will be strongly considered.

Feel free to email me if you're interested.

Update: The laptop's been sold, and to none other than my good friend Deb. She was also kind enough to take a heavy old 17-inch CRT off my hands, though I did have to carry it to the car for her.
