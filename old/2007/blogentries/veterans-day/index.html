Veteran's Day

11/11/2005 03:15:00 PM

Veteran's Day this year is likely to bring up discussions of the wars abroad, political arguments, and even some heated words. I'd just like to remind everyone that regardless of your political views or feelings about the current deployments, the men and women of the US armed forces, now or in the past, are to be thanked and admired. I've got more than one friend serving, and I couldn't be more grateful—we're talking about men and women of all ages, working harder than anyone else I know, and doing jobs most of us are unable or unwilling to do. They all have my respect and admiration; I hope you'll share that sentiment.
