Free iTunes Downloads

5/09/2006 05:58:00 PM

As requested by the Free iTunes Downloads blog, I'm happy to give them a little shout out. I've been using iTunes since I got my shiny new iPod a few months ago, and absolutely adore putting tons of metadata in my MP3s. (Yes, really.) Not too long ago, I realized that Apple updates the iTunes music store with one or two free tracks (and sometimes even videos) from time to time—it wasn't hard to check once a week or so what there was, but I was always afraid I'd miss such a “bargain”. Thanks to Free iTunes Downloads, though, I don't have to check on my own. Subscribe to the feed in Google Reader (or your favorite RSS aggregator), and you're set.

Yep, the “purchases” are free, but for some reason you still get an emailed receipt. Oh, and, yeah, they're “protected” (that is, restricted) AAC format, but I hear there are ways around that….
