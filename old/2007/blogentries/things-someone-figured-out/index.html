Things Someone Figured Out

6/17/2006 09:57:00 PM

For some enlightening reading (for a change), I happened upon some guy named Rob's Things I Figured Out, a short list of concepts no one seems to teach, but that this enterprising young man figured out on his own. Even more enlightening, though, are a few of his reader contributions. Here a just a few of my favorites:
  • It is best to buy the best shoes, the best car, and the best bed that you can afford. You will be spending about 100% of your life in one or the other of them, so you ought to be comfortable.
  • You can indeed get a stitch through a thumbnail. And that novocaine shot likely didn't penetrate far enough, so brace yourself.
  • No matter how hot she is, someone, somewhere is sick of her shit.
