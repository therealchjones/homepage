My Eyes! My Eyes!!

3/08/2007 05:02:00 PM

I've said before how disgusted I am by Los Angeles, “The City of Angels” (well, I didn't find it to be that exactly). Still, little had prepared me for what I saw today. I parked my car near the USC Health Sciences campus on my way to an afternoon in the Surgical Skills Lab. I got out, and noticed that the SUV behind me was a-rockin'. Despite the cliché, I approached, and was greeted with the site of a man's hairy ass bouncing up and down in the back seat. Luckily, that was all I saw, before shouting “Dude, what the hell are you doing?”, knowing full well what he was doing. He turned around (well, turned his head, which was actually preferable) and looked at me. I said “This is a public place!”, and he shyly responded, “okay, okay, we're leaving”. A couple minutes later, a clothed woman crawls into the driver's seat and they drive away.

Wow. Seriously, wow. Other than New Orleans, and even then only during Mardi Gras, where does this happen? We're on a city street, parked at a meter, about a block from a public school. Wow. I seriously couldn't believe it.

It's really no wonder none of my interviewers in the Midwest or on the east coast were surprised that I might actually want to leave LA.

It does kind of makes you wonder if there was an argument about how much money to put in the meter. The guy's all “Oh, I only have a dime, I guess that'll do”, while his little mistress is dismayed that those seven minutes of delight probably won't put her over the top.

As I said to a couple of my friends afterwards, if I'm going to be walking down the street and seeing two people having sex, they'd better be two chicks, and they'd better be hot.

Oh, and, just to add insult to injury, for obvious reasons I forgot to put any change in my parking meter. A forty-dollar ticket was my wonderful ending to the story. I'd consider challenging it on extenuating circumstances, but I can't imagine any judge believing the story. Or maybe I'm just afraid he would.
