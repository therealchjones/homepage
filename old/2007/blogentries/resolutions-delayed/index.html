Resolutions, Delayed

1/01/2006 05:14:00 PM

New Year's resolution time. Note that because of (or thanks to) my anniversary falling on January 1 each year, Lindsey asked last year that I start all resolutions on January 2. Seems I get pretty cranky the first few of days of any diet, and a diet always seems to be one of my resolutions (see the first couple below). Not surprising, I guess, that she wouldn't want me to be particularly fussy on our anniversary. Again, then, that precious mantra: The-diet-starts-tomorrow.

In no particular order:

  • Exercise (aerobic) at least thirty minutes every day
  • Ingest less than two thousand calories every day
  • Study at least one hour every day

That's it. Call days may be painful, but I'm really going to try. Note that these are the “absolute” resolutions; I've tried to limit myself to three that I will always do and not just try to do. That said, there are several things that didn't make the list but which I'll try to do anyway, somewhat more subjective things like “keep better track of my money” and “do better at studying my non-medical interests”. We'll see how well those go.

For now, though, one more night of junk food and sitting in front of the computer and/or the television. One more night of a happy new year—the pain starts whenever I wake up in the morning.
