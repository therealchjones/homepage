Getting Connected

2/03/2007 04:03:00 PM

As med school winds down and I get ready to move on (both professionally and geographically), I've been reflecting a bit and continuing to build the “Christian Jones” brand. To that extent, I registered ChristianJones.name and set up a simple nameplate site there; I've also become quite an avid fan of LinkedIn. It's at first yet another social networking site, but this one allows minimal (if any) customization of any home page you might have, and is geared more toward the professional who wants to keep in touch with contacts and get and give recommendations from and to them. Really all I'm looking for, and, if nothing else, it's encouraged me to reconnect with some of my old Harvey Mudd pals.
