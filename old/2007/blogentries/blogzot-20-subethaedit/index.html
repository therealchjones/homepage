BlogZOT 2.0 - SubEthaEdit

4/25/2006 05:20:00 PM

Oh, yes, god forbid I plug something on my blog for the sole purpose of personal gain. Still, when MacZOT did their first BlogZOT a couple of weeks ago, I got a couple free licenses for AppZapper, a decent (IMHO) little utility for anyone who experiments at all with their Mac. Now, BLOGZOT 2.0 on MacZOT.com is promising to deliver SubEthaEdit from CodingMonkeys, a piece interesting collaborative-editing software that many Mac business users swear by. (Actually, since I'm not one, maybe this isn't all for personal gain. Still, I'm always up for free stuff, especially when it's quality; and if this works (as BlogZOT 1.0 did), that means that MacZOT and TheCodingMonkeys will award $105,000 in Mac software to the participants and up to 3,000 other people who ask nicely. Not bad, eh?
