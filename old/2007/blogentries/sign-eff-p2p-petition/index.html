Sign the EFF P2P Petition

5/03/2006 08:03:00 PM

I listen to music. I don't believe sharing it should put you at risk for losing thousands of dollars, and, as I've mentioned before, many artists don't think so, either. Now the Electronic Frontier Foundation is taking the electronic signatures of 100,000 people who think similarly, and hope this will in some way influence policy in this country. While I'm uncertain about the effect it will have, I'm happy to add my name; why not sign the EFF P2P petition yourself? And, hey, if you want to keep fighting for the cause, it doesn't hurt to become an EFF member; “student” level starts at $15.
