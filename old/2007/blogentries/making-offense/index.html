Making offense

11/09/2005 03:33:00 PM

Pediatrics is boring. “Mind-numbing” would be charitable. To top it off, I'm one of the few on my rotation who won't be spending a day in the pediatric ER, quite possibly the only experience I could find interesting here. Grrr.

Four of us spent four hours this morning at a day-care center. Oh, yes, learning “normal child development” for four hours. Eight to noon. I started looking at my watch at about 8:45.

I don't have any clue why, but the kids liked me. They kept climbing on me, sneezing on me, and even trying to sit on my lap. I didn't think that last would be appropriate, then I started being concerned about doing other inappropriate things: talking about herpes; cursing; making a giant penis out of Play-Doh. Who knew what crazy things I might accidentally do?

Longest four hours I've spent this year.
