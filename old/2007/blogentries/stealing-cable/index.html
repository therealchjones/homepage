Stealing Cable

1/29/2006 07:53:00 PM

A nice little reciprocal shout-out here: a buddy of mine named Faze and some of his pals put together a nice little show on a semi-regular basis called “Stealing Cable”;I'm happy to be a listener and I think you should be, too. It sounds just like your friends sitting around talking about TV, if your friends actually know something about TV. Their podcast is on iTunes, so search for it in the music store, subscribe, and listen up.
