Wacky News

3/19/2007 10:07:00 AM

Wow, another new irregular feature here at ~chjones! I know all my reader is thrilled.

Alright, just a couple of interesting news stories plucked from MSNBC today. (Yes, I read MSNBC. Yes, it's a joint venture of NBC and Microsoft. It's still my choice for news—and as long as there is actually a choice, I won't complain about it… much.)

Die, and get a free upgrade to first class!
An elderly woman died after take-off on a flight from Delhi to London; British Airways flight attendants were kind enough to move her from coach to first class at no additional charge! I was particularly horrimused at the fact that her body kept falling to the floor, and had to be propped up with pillows. So that's why I can never get a pillow on a long flight.
“Bong Hits 4 Jesus” goes to the Supreme Court
A kid after my own heart unfurled a banner reading “Bong Hits 4 Jesus” just to be “provocative”. It was during a school-sponsored event, but outside the school itself. That was a long time ago, and since then his father's been fired and the student himself was caught (shockingly) selling pot. I'm particularly interested in the fact that Ken Starr (not the rapper) is representing the school that suspended the kid, and that there doesn't seem to be an argument about freedom of religion here.
