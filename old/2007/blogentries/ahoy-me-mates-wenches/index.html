Ahoy, Me Mates & Wenches!

9/19/2006 10:15:00 AM

Arr! Today be Talk Like A Pirate Day! 'Tis no small thing to be mindful of when I be makin' surgical rounds, but I bin threatenin' to keel-haul the scurvy dogs who bin tellin' me to speak normal. Ha-harr, they be lucky I#39;ve not sent them on to Davy Jones' locker.

Aye, but 'tis not such a glorious day for all pirates—them in Sweden bin kept out th' Parliament by the landlubber voters who be livin' there. 'Tis a travesty fit to send even the most hopeful amongst we into the salty brine.
