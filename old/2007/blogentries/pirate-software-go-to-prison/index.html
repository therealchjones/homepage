Pirate Software, Go to Prison

10/05/2006 03:16:00 PM

Perhaps surprisingly, I have no problem with the above declaration. News.com.com has an interesting little story about a man getting 7 years and a $5.4 million fine for copying and illegally selling software. Yup, that's illegal, and I have no problem with the given punishment. Some may find that at odds with my advocating “information liberation” (not “piracy”), but I've never suggested one should sell something which should be free in the first place. I suppose there may be different definitions of “profiting” from one's crime, but let's at least try to keep the hypocrisy out of it, huh?
