Student Tips & Tricks

9/30/2006 04:05:00 AM

It's a little late to call this a “back to school” post, but I've noticed a couple of pretty sweet lists lately I wanted to link to. The first is a decent, if unsurprising, list from the electrical engineering department at Cal Poly which humbly calls itself How to Be a Student. The author presents several basics on getting your “attitude” as a student right. Though it notes in a few places that the document is geared toward undergraduates, there's no reason the points are limited to them.

More entertaining, more insightful, and more useful to those as jaded as myself is Steve Dutch's Top Ten No Sympathy Lines. I've heard—and in the past, probably used—almost all of these, and it's nice to see a real point-by-point analysis of them. You're paying a ridiculous amount for your education—don't think that means you deserve more than to learn.
